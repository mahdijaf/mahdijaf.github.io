About

I am Dr. Mahdee Jodayree, a researcher and educator in Computer Science with expertise in federated learning, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and large-scale distributed systems. I completed my PhD at McMaster University in the Department of Computing & Software, where my research focused on distributed machine learning, secure aggregation, and performance optimization.
