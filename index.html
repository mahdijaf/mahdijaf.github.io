About

I am Dr. Mahdee Jodayree, a researcher and educator in Computer Science. My work spans federated learning, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and distributed systems. I completed my PhD at McMaster University (Department of Computing & Software), focusing on secure, bandwidth-aware federated learning and performance optimization.
